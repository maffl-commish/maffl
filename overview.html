What Is MAFFL HQ?

MAFFL HQ pulls the whole league into one place — the rulebook, the records, the credit system, the prize pool, and the bragging rights. Think of it as league headquarters: not where you set your lineup, but where everything else lives.

  • Updated regularly all season. Weekly scores and standings refresh as games finish, credit balances and the registry update as credits are earned, and the rulebook, prizes, and all-time records are kept current by the Commish.
  • A complement to ESPN — not a replacement. Keep using the ESPN league site(s) for the live stuff: scoring, rosters, lineups, waivers, and trades. MAFFL HQ adds everything ESPN can't — the rulebook, the credit system, power rankings, all-time history, head-to-head rivalries, the prize ledger, and league polls.
  • One tap from anywhere. Add MAFFL HQ to your phone's Home Screen and it opens full-screen like an app. Every page below is a tap away.

👋 Heads up: this welcome tour is part of launch and will step aside about a month in, once everyone's settled into the site. The pages themselves stay right where they are.
